Jakub Takáč

I am a postdoc at the University of Trento working in the group of Andrea Marchese. Before, I was a PhD. student at the University of Warwick under the supervision of David Bate. I work in the field of geometric measure theory. Previously I worked in function spaces and interpolation theory - these are the topics of my Master and Bachelor theses. Complete list of my publications is available on arXiv.

Professional interests:

Geometric measure theory in Euclidean and metric spaces: differentiability of Lipschitz maps; 'geometric' and 'analytic' structure of sets and measures; functional analysis of spaces of Lipschitz functions; Hausdorff measure and dimension; Metric currents

Upcoming events:

Geometric Measure Theory: unraveling the structure of singularities (Trento)
Geometric measure theory and Metric geometry (University of Warwick)
